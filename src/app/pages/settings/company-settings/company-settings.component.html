General

Allowed email domain

Protect your environment by restricting access to only those colleagues (email address domains) which match one of the allowed email domains. Sample: For abc@company.com input domain as company.com You can specify one or more email domains. If a user attempts to invite a colleague with a different domain, than this will not be possible.

Fraud protection

Unreliable email domains

Set email domains that your company considers to be a potential risk and requires attention. For instance public domains like gmail.com or icloud.com It does not block usage, but hints user during creation of the tender in case groups are invited with an Email address ending with specified domains.
